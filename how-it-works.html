What Happens Behind the Scenes

Our technology works seamlessly to deliver accurate results in seconds

1. Location Intelligence

Our geocoding engine cross-references your input with Rwanda's cadastral database, identifying exact parcel boundaries, zone classification, and administrative details.

Covers all 30 districts

2. Gazette Data Integration

We pull official coefficients from Rwanda Land Authority's gazette, applying the correct multipliers based on zone type (urban, rural, agricultural, marshland).

Updated quarterly

3. Market Analysis & Adjustments

Our AI analyzes recent transactions, infrastructure developments, and demand trends to adjust base valuations for current market conditions.

10+ years of historical data

4. Comprehensive Report Generation

You receive a detailed PDF report with current value, historical trends, comparable properties, and investment recommendations.

Downloadable + shareable
